Royel Enfild

Key characteristics

Retro and classic design: Royal Enfield motorcycles are famous for their vintage look, often featuring round headlights, a classic tank shape, and a retro-analogue dashboard. The brand has a long history, with the Bullet being the longest-running production motorcycle model.Modern technology: While embracing classic styling, many newer models incorporate modern features for an improved riding experience. Examples include all-LED lighting, ABS, and a digital LCD display for features like fuel level and gear position. Some bikes also include turn-by-turn navigation. Unique riding experience: The brand is often associated with a distinct riding feel, characterized by the sound of the engine and a focus on the riding posture. For instance, the Classic 350 is noted for its relaxed and comfortable ergonomics.
